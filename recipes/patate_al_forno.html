Roasted potatoes recipe

The following recipe is a translation took from GialloZafferano.

Recipe description

Simple recipe for roasted potatoes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes 1kg
  • Garlic 2 cloves
  • Fine Salt to taste
  • Rosemary 2 sprigs
  • Extra virgin olive oil 30g
  • Thyme 2 sprigs

For greasing the pan

  • Extra virgin olive oil to taste

Preparation

  1. Wash and peel potatoes, divide them in half and then into quarters, finally in small cubes.
  2. Put a water pan to boil and boil cubes for 5 min
  3. Drain the cubes with a skimmer and transfer them in a bowl.
  4. Add thyme, oil and salt and stir.
  5. Preheat oven at 200 degree Celsius with inside a baking tray that will be used for potatoes.
  6. When desired temp is reached, extract the tray and place some oil in the bottom.
  7. Add the potatoes, rosemary sprigs and two cloves of garlic without peeling them.
  8. Bake the potatoes at 200 degree Celsius for 1 hour or until the potatoes are crispy and golden. Every 20 min mix them to assure an even cooking.
  9. Remove garlic, let potatoes cool a bit and serve immediately!
