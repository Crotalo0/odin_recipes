Lasagna recipe

The following recipe is a translation took from GialloZafferano.

Recipe description

The "Lasagne alla bolognese" are the typical and standard lasagna recipe in Italy.

Lasagna is made with ragu', besciamella and grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese.

Ingredients

For pasta sheets

  • Durum wheat semolina 350g
  • Flour 00 150g
  • Spinach 250g
  • Eggs 2
  • Egg yolks 3

For ragu'

  • bovine macinated meat 300g
  • Bacon 150g
  • Carrots 50g
  • Golden onions 50g
  • Red wine 100g
  • Tomato puree 300g
  • celery 50g
  • Vegetal broth - needed quantity
  • Extravirgin olive oil 1 spoon
  • Salt - needed quantity
  • Black pepper - needed quantity

For besciamella

  • Butter 70g
  • Flour 00 70g
  • Milk 1l
  • Salt
  • nutmeg

For condiments

  • Butter
  • Parmigiano Reggiano DOP 270g

Preparation

Starting with ragu'

  1. Prepare the broth, cut bacon in small pieces. Cut celery, carrots and onion finely and put them apart.
  2. In a pan put a bit of oil and the bacon, when bacon is golden add celery, carrots, and onion. Let cook for 6 min then add the meat.
  3. Add wine, when completely evaporated add tomato puree. Cook for 2 hours and turn it.
  4. Add some broth to the ragu', close lid and cook for at least 2 hours again. Turn some times and add broth if needed.

Pasta's turn

  1. Put spinach in a pot, with a bit of water, covered with a lid.
  2. cook them until they are withered. ca. 5-6 min.
  3. Remove excess water, let them cool
  4. Put them in a mixer and obtain a puree. We need 100g of it.
  5. Put flour mixed with semolina on a mixing table forming a fountain or a volcano.
  6. put in the middle spinach and eggs and egg yolks.
  7. move the flour from sides to center and work it until the dough is ready.
  8. Take the omogeneous dough, wrap in plastic and let sit for 30 min.

Besciamella's turn

  1. In a small pot cook milk witouth reaching ebollition, and melt butter in another pan
  2. Add flour to butter and cook for some min until obtaining a golden mix and then add in three times milk.Dont forget to turn everything very well.
  3. Add salt and nutmeg, then when everything is creamy besciamella is ready. Put it in a small bowl anc cover with plastic paper.

Pasta manipulation

  1. After the 30 min, take a piece of pasta and make the tipical rectangular shaped pasta size.Work in steps with a machine to reach 1mm goal.
  2. Take 30x20cm rectangles from the pasta previously worked.
  3. Take a lid with water and salt, when boiling cook 1 rectangular pasta piece per time letting it cook for 30-40 seconds.
  4. Put the cooked pasta on a towel to remove water. Do the same with outer pasta pieces without letting them touch.

Composition Time

  1. Wen ragu' is cooked, we are ready to create our lasagna.
  2. Take a 30x40cm buttered baking tray add besciamella layer, ragu' and then first pasta piece.
  3. Continue in this order but for newer layers add also grated Parmigiano.
  4. PRoceed until 5 layers. After last pasta piece add ragu' and cover it completely.
  5. Add grated PArmigiano and butter cubes.
  6. Cook in preheated oven at 170 degree for 40 min.
